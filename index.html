My Approach

Lessons

Lessons can be as structured or as informal as you like. No two students are alike and I believe in custom tailoring your lessons to your specific needs. I also promise that lessons will be highly enjoyable and interesting. Plenty of professional references available.

Students

My teaching approach: I've had many years of private teaching experience including teaching at the college level. Some of my students have gone on to major in music and are now professional musicians themselves.

Your Success

I like to find out what my students' goals are from day one and help them succeed in an enjoyable way. No two students are alike and I believe in custom-tailoring my lessons to each student's needs.
